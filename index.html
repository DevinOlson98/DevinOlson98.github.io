Welcome to the UGA Liverpool FC Fan Club!

Please Use This Site For Keeping Up to Date With Liverpool FC!

This page is for all of the Liverpool supporters at the University of Georgia. Using this page will reference you to other LFC Fan pages, scores of recent games, and other related pages.

We all know that Liverpool is the greatest club in the world and we should celebrate that together. We love to watch everything about Liverpool. Whether it’s Salah running down the wing, Mane cutting in and scoring, Firmino scoring no-look tap ins, or Van Dijk making crunching tackles.

But it’s not just the players that make the club so great, it’s the fans as well. This is what this page is for, to make a page where all UGA and Liverpool fans can cheer on the reds. Hopefully this page will feel like a big Klopp hug after the end of a hard fought game. Please share this page with all of your friends and enjoy supporting Liverpool from Athens!

This is really a great website!
-Devin Olson
